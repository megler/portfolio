A quick overview

This is what Twitter Clone will do

Features
  • User Authentication
  • Backend SQL database
  • SPA functionality with Javascript
  • Custom views based on user ID/logged in status
  • Ability to follow/unfollow other users
  • Ability to like/unlike tweets
  • Pagination once the tweet list exceeds 10 tweets
  • Custom gravatars for registered users
  • A logged in user can see their choice of:
    • All posts
    • Just their posts
    • Just the posts of people they are following
